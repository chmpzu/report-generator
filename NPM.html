3) Preview

Formula logic: Sales Order Total = Total Amount Paid - Total Tax Price - Total Shipping Paid. If Total Amount Paid is blank and Sales Order Total has a value, Total Amount Paid will copy Sales Order Total. Merchant / List Fee = Sales Order Total × 0.13. Total Cost = Total Supplier Cost + SS Shipping Price + Merchant / List Fee. Net profit = Sales Order Total - Total Cost. NPM = Net profit / Sales Order Total.
